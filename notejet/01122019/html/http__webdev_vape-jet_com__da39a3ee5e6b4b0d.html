Innovative Hardware

Machine vision alignment system allows you to fill cartridges directly in shipping trays, without the need to transfer to a jig for alignment. Simply unbox the cartridges, place on the Vape-Jet, and click the dispense button. The Vape-Jet will align to every cartridge before filling, without any operator intervention.

Our state-of-the-art laboratory calibrated and certified pump can achieve the fastest possible dispense times. Programmable to dispense any volume from .001 ml to 5 ml.

Three fully independent, electrically heated, PID controlled, and software configurable heat-zones allow for precise temperature control. Holding temperature, pump temperature, and dispensing needle temperature are all independently controlled. Temperature variations are eliminated using PID control.

The Vape-Jet system can be fully cleaned in place using your choice of solvents. Simply fill the reservoir with your cleaning solution, and run the cleaning wizard through the GUI. The full cleanup process takes less than 10 minutes, and is almost fully software automated.

The Vape-Jet fluid path is made entirely from PTFE, PCTFE, Viton, Borosilicate glass, stainless steel, and ceramic. Swagelok fitting are used for all connections, and our pumping valve is precision machined from ceramics.

The Vape-Jet is the smartest filling device on the planet. Using collected cartridge data and photos, our nueral network can detect defects and contaminants before filling, as well as providing tracibility down to individual cartridge verification.

Powerful Software

The Vape-Jet system is capable of saving 1,000 different configurations. A master of efficiency, the Vape-Jet system allows each configuration to loaded or updated as needed. Switching between different profiles is simple - and accommodates different product attributes and cartridge styles.

The Vape-Jet eliminates the need for costly and time consuming manual labor processes, and reduces your labor costs. Using our patent pending machine vision alignment system, you can dispense directly into foam shipping trays. The machine vision system is fully software configurable, and is compatible with a wide variety of cartridges. Optional center-post avoidance detects the optimal fill location for every cartridge, and decreases labor needed for pre-inspection/alignment.

Batch tracking and traceability are vitally important in today’s rapidly changing environment. Vape-Jet’s robust reporting and inspection capabilities are included out-of-the-box. Cartridge fill operations, and pre and post fill inspection photos can be recorded and viewed as necessary.

Help and support is built directly into the Vape-Jet interface. The GUI includes a live-chat window that connects you directly with the Vape-Jet engineer support team. The Vape-Jet system can be operated from any modern device, including desktops, laptops, tablets and phones. Multiple Vape-Jet systems can be run from a single device - allowing a single operator to keep up to five machines going at full capacity.
