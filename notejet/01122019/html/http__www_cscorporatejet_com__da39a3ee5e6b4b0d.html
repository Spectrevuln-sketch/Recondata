THE GAME - CHANGER

From the team that created the world’s first Boeing BBJ 787 VVIP aircraft comes a new

large-cabin Corporate Jet that is way ahead of its competitors . . .

NEW COMPANY

CS Corporate Jet is in the business of designing, building and certifying large corporate jets for discerning clients worldwide.

We are jointly owned by four of the most highly-respected companies in this field: Camber Aviation Management  (cabin concept initiator and project manager), Flying Colours (engineering, integration and certification), F/LIST (cabin engineering and fabrication) and Pierrejean International (detailed cabin design).

NEW IDEAS

Our team challenges the concept that transforming an airliner into a large corporate jet must be complex. We offer our clients

a stress-free experience in configuring a modern large cabin corporate jet to reflect that person’s individual requirements and

tastes, without the significant risk, cost and effort that conventional airliner conversions require.

DEMONSTRATED SUCCESS

Each of our partners have a proven track record in designing and executing complex aviation projects that have established bench- marks in the corporate aviation industry.  From the Bombardier Challenger to the Global, Airbus and Boeing single and twin aisle airliners, we have been involved in some of the most challenging and innovative cabin designs flying.

THE ATTAINABLE LARGE CORPORATE JET

We have developed a state-of-the-art Corporate Jet, that sets a new benchmark in large cabin design and customisation, operating economics and flexibility, as well as enhanced value and lower life-cycle costs compared to its competitors. „Our aircraft is equipped with the latest in high-speed communications and high definition entertainment systems, all integrated into a comfortable and welcoming cabin.”
