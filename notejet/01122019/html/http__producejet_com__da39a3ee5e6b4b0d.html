Providing a full turn-key solution
for your box coding needs

The Produce Jet system uses state of the art vision recognition software and hardware to accurately identify and label product. Our system provides ease of use and flexibility to accommodate the PTI and federal standards for box coding. We also provide value add features such as worker performance tally, system reporting, payroll integration, and database integration.

State of the art hardware

Performance and reliability are important to our customers. That is why we use a high-speed gigabit Dalsa GigE camera paired with the robust ID Technologies applicator.
